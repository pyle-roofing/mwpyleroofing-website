About M.W. Pyle Roofing

Family-run, locally trusted, and built on over 35 years of roofing experience across Eastbourne and East Sussex.

Roofers in Eastbourne and East Sussex

M.W. Pyle Roofing provides roof repairs, new roofs, flat roofing, guttering, and fascia & soffit replacement to homes and businesses across Eastbourne and the wider southeast of England. Every quotation is free, with no obligation.

We're a family-run business with over 35 years of experience in our field, and that experience shows in the standard of work we deliver — from a single repair to a full re-roof.

M.W. Pyle Roofing Ltd welcomes regular inspections of work undertaken, whether by surveyors, architects, or homeowners themselves — while work is in progress, or on completion. Speed, efficiency, and a same-day service wherever possible are essential to how we operate, with most jobs returning a quote within five working days.
