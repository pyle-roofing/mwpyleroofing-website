Our roofing services

M.W. Pyle Roofing offers a wide range of services to suit your every need — from a single repair to a full re-roof.

Specialists in pitched roofing

We specialise in all varieties of pitched roof types, including Natural and Synthetic Slating, Peg Tiling, and Concrete and Clay, priding ourselves on our skilled leadwork. We also offer a number of other specialist services, detailed below.

Slating & Tiling

Natural and synthetic slating, peg tiling, and concrete or clay tiling — installed and repaired to a high standard, built to withstand East Sussex weather.

Guttering

Supply, repair and replacement of guttering to keep water flowing away from your home and protect your fascia, soffit and walls from damage.

Fascia & Soffit Replacement

Modern, low-maintenance fascia and soffit boards fitted to improve both the look and the weatherproofing of your roofline.

Single Ply & Liquid Plastic Flat Roofing

Durable flat roofing solutions for extensions, garages and outbuildings, using single ply membrane or liquid plastic systems.

Chimney Removal

Safe, fully made-good chimney removal and re-roofing where a chimney is no longer needed or has become structurally unsound.

Bird Prevention

Proofing against pigeons and gulls nesting under tiles or in guttering, protecting your roof structure and keeping it clear.

Velux Window Installation

Supply and fitting of Velux roof windows to bring natural light into loft conversions and upper-floor rooms.

Leadwork

Skilled leadwork to flashings, valleys and chimneys — one of the trickiest parts of any roof to get right, and one of ours to specialise in.
